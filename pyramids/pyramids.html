Pyramids Valley Football Club

also known as "Al-Ahram Club", is an Egyptian football club that was established in 2008 under the name Al-Assuiti Sport before selling its rights to the former owner Turki Al-Sheikh in 2018, and its headquarters are in the Air Defense Stadium in Cairo. The club is currently playing in the Egyptian Premier League, which is the highest league in Egyptian football leagues.

Assuit sport period

Assiuti Sport Club was established in the year 2008 in Assiut. The start of the team was in the same year by participating in the fourth division, where he rose after two seasons to the third section in which two seasons continued until he climbed at the end of the 2013 season for the second division for one season and then went up to the Premier League for the first time since its foundation in the 2014-15 season after winning the Ascending Qualifiers. Then, the relegation returned two seasons in a row to the Egyptian League, the second division, until he climbed again to Al-Mumtaz in the 2017-2018 season.

Turki Al Sheikh Acquisition: 19-19-2018

In the summer of 2018, Turki Al Sheikh, the head of the Saudi Sports Authority, bought the club, his name, and his license from its owner Mahmoud Al-Assuiti, in addition to renting the Asyuti resort to be the club’s headquarters, where the club’s name changed from “Al-Assuiti Sport” to “Al-Ahram Club” or “Pyramids” ". On June 28, 2018, it was announced that former Egyptian Al-Ahly coach Hossam Al-Badri will take over the club while Ahmed Hassan will be the team's spokesman and supervisor, in addition to appointing Hadi Khashaba as football director and coach Botafogo Regatas Alberto Valentim will take over the team's training. The fans of the Egyptian fans of Al-Ahly club against the owner of the Pyramids Club Turki Al-Sheikh on September 24, 2018 caused Al-Sheikh to announce one day later the end of his investment experience in Egypt and thus the end of the club and his channel and the sale of contracts for his players.

Selling it to Salem Al Shamsi 2019

Selling the club for the first time On February 22, 2019, the club announced in an official statement on its Facebook page, the club, that the club had been sold to an Emirati investor, Salem Saeed Al Shamsi, after liquidating the sports investments of former owner Turki Al Sheikh. Despite this, Al-Sheikh regained the club after only one day. Sell the club again On July 4 of 2019, the club's pages on social media announced that Salem Al-Shamsi purchased the club again from its Saudi owner, Turki Al-Sheikh.
